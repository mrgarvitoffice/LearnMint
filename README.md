## About The Project

# 🌱 LearnMint

**LearnMint** is a futuristic, AI-powered learning platform designed to redefine how students study and absorb knowledge. With the power of **Google Gemini** and a sleek, responsive UI built on **Next.js**, LearnMint offers a personalized and interactive educational experience — automating material creation, enabling contextual learning, and maximizing retention like never before. !

> Built by **MrGarvit**, LearnMint is not just a study tool — it’s your AI-powered academic assistant.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📘 Unified AI Material Generation  
Create complete study kits from any topic in seconds:

- 📝 **In-depth Notes**  
  Markdown-formatted, well-structured notes with headings, lists, and AI-generated images.  
- 🎯 **Interactive Quizzes**  
  Auto-generated MCQs and short answers, each with detailed explanations to reinforce learning.  
- 📚 **Smart Flashcards**  
  Instant flashcards for rapid revision and memorization.

### 🧪 Custom Test Creation Lab  
Build tailored tests by combining topics, selecting difficulty levels, setting timers, and using custom notes.

### 🤖 Advanced AI Chatbot  
Converse with intelligent AI personas. Upload **text, images, PDFs, audio, or video** to ask questions and receive contextual answers.

### 🎧 Audio Factory *(by MrGarvit)*  
Generate summaries from **text, image, PDF, or flashcards** — delivered in **audio format** for hands-free learning.

### 🛠️ Integrated Toolkit & Resource Library  
- 🔬 Scientific Calculator  
- 📏 Unit Converter  
- 🔎 Integrated YouTube & Google Books Search

### 🌐 Personalization & Accessibility  
- 🌙 Dark/Light Mode  
- 🔠 Adjustable Font Sizes  
- 🌍 Multi-language Interface

## 🎨 Customization

Want to tailor LearnMint to your needs? Here's how:

- 🎨 **Styling & Theme**: Modify colors and styles in `src/app/globals.css` and `tailwind.config.ts`.
- 🧠 **AI Prompts**: Adjust AI behavior by editing prompt logic in `src/ai/flows/`.
- 🔤 **Fonts**: Change the global font in `src/app/layout.tsx`.
